Minecraft Enchantments Guide

Source: Minecraft Wiki

Armor

Helmet

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Protection IV or Projectile Protection IV
    or Blast Protection IV or Fire Protection IV
  • Respiration III
  • Aqua Affinity (Optional. Makes mining underwater faster.)
  • Thorns III (Optional. Consumes extra durability.)

Chestplate

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Protection IV or Projectile Protection IV
    or Blast Protection IV or Fire Protection IV
  • Thorns III (Optional. Consumes extra durability.)

Leggings

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Protection IV or Projectile Protection IV
    or Blast Protection IV or Fire Protection IV
  • Thorns III (Optional. Consumes extra durability.)
  • Swift Sneak III

Boots

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Protection IV or Projectile Protection IV
    or Blast Protection IV or Fire Protection IV
  • Thorns III (Optional. Consumes extra durability.)
  • Depth Strider III or Frost Walker II (Depth Strider overall preferred.)
  • Soul Speed III (Optional. Consumes extra Durability.)

Weapons

Sword

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Sharpness V (For general use.) or Smite V (For fighting undead mobs.)
    or Bane of Arthropods V (For fighting Arthropods.)
  • Sweeping Edge III [Java Edition only]
    (Deals more damage to multiple mobs while standing still, useful for XP farms, though you may accidentally hit your pets if they're too close.)
  • Looting III
  • Fire Aspect II
    (Optional. Makes endermen a hassle to fight, since they teleport everywhere while on fire; hostile mobs that are set on fire set you on fire on melee attacks; cooks food dropped by cows, pigs, hoglins, sheep, chickens, rabbits, cod, and salmon even in rain.)
  • Knockback II
    (Optional. Can make drowneds and skeletons a hassle to fight; useful against creepers.)

Axe

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Efficiency V
  • Sharpness V (For general use.) or Smite V (For fighting undead mobs.)
    or Bane of Arthropods V (For fighting Arthropods.)
  • Silk Touch (useful for melons, bookshelves and beehives)
    or Fortune III (only slightly useful for melons)

Bow

  • Infinity or Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Power V
  • Flame
  • Punch II (Optional) (knockback)

Crossbow

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Quick Charge III
  • Piercing IV

Trident

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Loyalty III and Channeling or only Riptide III
  • Impaling V

Tools

Hoe

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Efficiency V
  • Silk Touch or Fortune III Silk Touch is useful for obtaining leaves or blocks in the Sculk family. Fortune is useful for obtaining apples and saplings from leaves, or obtaining more Potato or Carrot from Crops.

    or Fortune III (only slightly useful for melons and mushroom blocks)

  • Fortune III (useful for ores)
